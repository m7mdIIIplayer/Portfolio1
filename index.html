About me

I'm Laila Brown, I've been photographing, it seems, all my life (actually 6 years). My work is what makes me me, what makes me feel euphoric, meaningful, happy. First of all I like to show you as beautiful as I see you. My dream is that every person who comes to me for a photo shoot sees how beautiful, poignant, sexy and repeatable he is.

A frequent question of my clients: "How can you see me so beautifully? Is it really me?" I broadcast and capture only how I feel you. Photography is much more than just a visual representation of you. I'm trying to convey your soul.
